# DEC-002 — Visibility Before Optimization

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: Accepted

## Decision

VOYO will prioritize transportation visibility and communication before route optimization.

## Context

Founder interviews repeatedly identified:

* Uncertainty
* Delays
* Lack of visibility
* Communication gaps

as the primary customer pain points.

## Decision

Priority:

1. Visibility
2. Communication
3. Reliability

Later:

* Route optimization
* AI scheduling
* Predictive routing

## Consequences

P1 focuses on passenger status visibility and communication workflows.
