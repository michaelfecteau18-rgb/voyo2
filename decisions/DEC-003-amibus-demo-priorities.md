# DEC-003 — Amibus Demo Strategy

Date: 2026-06-23

Status: Accepted

## Decision

The immediate objective is a professional and honest demonstration for Amibus.

## Success Criteria

Demonstrate:

* Driver application
* Dispatcher dashboard
* Live GPS tracking
* Passenger status updates
* Transportation visibility

## Non-Goals

* Full route optimization
* Advanced AI features
* School transportation workflows
* Enterprise reporting

## Consequences

Development prioritizes demo readiness over feature breadth.
