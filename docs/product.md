# VOYO Product Definition

## Primary Users

### Dispatcher

Needs:

* Vehicle visibility
* Driver visibility
* Trip visibility
* Delay management

### Driver

Needs:

* Route guidance
* Passenger information
* Easy pickup and drop-off workflow

### Caregiver / Residence Staff

Needs:

* Know when transportation is arriving
* Know if transportation is delayed
* Know when passenger has been picked up

### Family Member

Needs:

* Know passenger status
* Know estimated arrival time
* Receive notifications

## Core Product Pillars

1. Visibility
2. Communication
3. Reliability
4. Simplicity

## What VOYO Is Not

* A complex ERP
* A payroll platform
* A fleet maintenance system

VOYO focuses on transportation visibility and communication first.
