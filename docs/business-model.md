# VOYO Business Model

## Mission

VOYO eliminates uncertainty in transportation.

---

## Core Value Proposition

VOYO helps transportation organizations:

* Reduce phone calls
* Improve visibility
* Improve communication
* Increase customer confidence

---

## Initial Target Market

Adapted Transportation

Examples:

* Amibus
* Mobility transportation providers
* Specialized transportation services

---

## Future Markets

School Transportation

Medical Transportation

Senior Transportation

Municipal Transportation

---

## Primary Buyer

Transportation Organizations

Potential Buyers:

* Transportation Managers
* Operations Managers
* Transportation Directors
* Transportation Companies

---

## End Users

* Dispatchers
* Drivers
* Caregivers
* Families

---

## Revenue Model

Monthly SaaS Subscription

Potential Pricing Models:

Option A:
Per Vehicle

Option B:
Per Active Driver

Option C:
Per Organization

## Success Metrics
