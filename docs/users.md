# VOYO User Profiles

## Dispatcher

Responsibilities:

* Monitor vehicles
* Manage delays
* Track active trips
* Respond to issues

Pain Points:

* Too many phone calls
* Lack of visibility
* Delayed vehicles
* Driver coordination

Goals:

* Know where vehicles are
* Reduce uncertainty
* Improve service quality

---

## Driver

Responsibilities:

* Follow assigned route
* Pick up passengers
* Drop off passengers
* Maintain schedule

Pain Points:

* Route confusion
* Last-minute changes
* Communication issues

Goals:

* Simple workflow
* Easy navigation
* Clear instructions

---

## Caregiver / Residence Staff

Responsibilities:

* Prepare residents
* Coordinate transportation
* Monitor arrivals

Pain Points:

* Waiting without information
* Delays
* Lack of updates

Goals:

* Know when transportation arrives
* Know when transportation leaves
* Reduce uncertainty

---

## Family Member

Pain Points:

* Not knowing where loved ones are
* Transportation delays
* Lack of communication

Goals:

* Visibility
* Notifications
* Peace of mind

---

## Transportation Organization

Goals:

* Reduce phone calls
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Improve operational efficiency
* Increase visibility

Success Metrics:

* Fewer complaints
* Faster communication
* Better customer experience
