# VOYO Vision

## Mission

VOYO eliminates uncertainty in transportation through real-time visibility, communication, and peace of mind.

## Problem

Today, transportation users often do not know:

* If transportation has been assigned
* When transportation will arrive
* Where the vehicle currently is
* Whether a passenger has been picked up
* Whether a passenger has arrived safely

This creates phone calls, frustration, delays, and uncertainty.

## Solution

VOYO provides real-time visibility to everyone involved in transportation:

* Dispatchers
* Drivers
* Caregivers
* Families
* Transportation organizations

## Core Promise

Nobody should ever need to ask:

"Where is my ride?"

## Long-Term Vision

VOYO becomes the visibility layer for transportation services.

Initial market:

* Adapted transportation

Future markets:

* School transportation
* Medical transportation
* Specialized transportation services

## Success Metrics

* Reduced phone calls
* Reduced uncertainty
* Increased customer satisfaction
* Increased transportation visibility
* Recurring SaaS revenue
